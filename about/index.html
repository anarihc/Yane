ABOUT YANE

More Than a Company.
A Framework for Growth.

Yane is a Sri Lankan holding company built on a simple belief — that great businesses, built patiently, compound into something extraordinary.

CREATE BUILD COMPOUND

Yane Enterprises was born out of a desire to build something that lasts. In an era of rapid change, we focus on the fundamentals: quality, resilience, and long-term value.

We are a Sri Lankan holding company with a global outlook. Our mission is to identify, build, and compound businesses that define the next generation of industry in our region.

We don't just invest capital — we invest time and strategy. Every company under the Yane umbrella is built from the ground up, ensuring its DNA aligns with our core pillars: Create, Build, and Compound.
