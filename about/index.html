01 — ORIGIN & MISSION

Yane Enterprises was born out of a desire to build something that lasts. In an era of rapid change, we focus on the fundamentals: quality, resilience, and long-term value.

We are a Sri Lankan holding company with a global outlook. Our mission is to identify, build, and compound businesses that define the next generation of industry in our region.

02 — OUR APPROACH

We don't just invest capital; we invest time and strategy. Every project under the Yane umbrella is built from the ground up, ensuring that its DNA aligns with our core pillars: Create, Build, and Compound.

From informatics to artisanal tea, our portfolio is diverse but unified by a single standard of excellence.
